Mosher-Jordan Hall was the dorm I lived in freshman year. I had a room on the 4th floor, and it was decent, for a freshman dorm at least. The room was small, but the common spaces were super nice and bougie, so I think I really lucked out.

This being where I lived, I was probably here the most out of all the other places. My go-to locations in Mosher-Jordan were the lounge on my floor with TV and armchairs, the main common room on the second floor with a grand piano and huge long table, and the two living rooms on the first floor with adjacent kitchens.
